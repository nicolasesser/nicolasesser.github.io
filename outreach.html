Astronomy on Tap (AoT) Montreal

AoT Montreal are held monthly by professional astronomers and in collaboration with several institutes (CRAQ, iREx and the Trottier Space Institute). Each event includes two short presentations by local researchers, trivia games with prizes for the winners, and offers a great opportunity to ask all your deep space questions, from black holes to aliens, to our large crew of passionate astronomers!

ESERO Belgium - Space in class

As a member of ESERO (The European Space Education Resource Office) project in Belgium, I performed outreach in several schools of Brussels on the topic of space, the solar system, and the planet Mars. The goal is to raise the interest of pupils in space/astrophysics and make them feel more comfortable and familiar with sciences in general.


Club M.A.R.S. President (2020-2022)

Former president of an association of scientific outreach (~100 members) centered around the planet Mars and missions dedicated to the exploration and study of the Red Planet in Toulouse, France.

  • Organisation weekly conferences on human space exploration and planetary astrophysics.
  • Management of scientific communication activities in Toulouse high-schools.
  • Mentoring scientific projects led by the Club M.A.R.S.

Space@YourService (website)

Founding member and former vice-president of an association promoting scientific communication about space and astronomy. Organisation of several events in Lausanne consisting in short talks on space sciences for all audiences:

  • Space Oddity. Invited speakers: Manu Stalport, Aurelien Verdier, and Emilie Hertig.
  • The Eagle has landed, 50 years of Man on the Moon. Invited speakers: Swiss astronaut Claude Nicollier and Dr. Apurva Oza.
  • From Earth to Outer Space. Invited speakers: Astronaut Claude Nicollier, Dr. Camille Pirat, Dr. Florian Gallien, and Dr. Daniel Angerhausen.
  • Aiming for the Moon. Invited speakers: Martine Harmel, George Meylan, and Dr. Daniel Angerhausen.
